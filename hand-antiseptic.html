Uni-Guard

Ready-to-use, fast-acting, alcoholic rub-in preparation for hygienic and surgical hand disinfection.

Characteristics

  • Prevents the proliferation of cutaneous bacteria that may cause skin infections
  • Reduces nosocomial infections through effective hand disinfection
  • Gentle to the skin due to pH-balanced formula
  • Bactericidal, tuberculocidal, fungicidal, and virucidal
  • Effective against all enveloped “blood-borne” viruses (including HBV and HIV)
  • Also effective against non-enveloped viruses like Adenovirus, Rotavirus, and Norovirus

Usage

Apply an adequate amount of Uni-Guard to the hands and rub thoroughly for hygienic or surgical disinfection. No need for water or towels. Quick-drying and leaves no sticky residue.

Caution

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. In case of eye contact, rinse immediately with plenty of water. Keep out of reach of children.
