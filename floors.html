Uni-Surf

Uni-Surf is a highly concentrated, broad-spectrum floor and surface disinfectant specially formulated for medical and dental use. It is effective against a wide range of microorganisms, including bacteria (such as TB), fungi, and both enveloped and non-enveloped viruses. Certified by the CEN (European Committee for Standardization), Uni-Surf is toxin- and aldehyde-free, biodegradable (as per OECD), and eco-friendly. Ideal for use with mopping or fogging machines, it dissolves blood, leaves a pleasant odor, and ensures a clean, safe environment without side effects for users.

Characteristics

  • Aldehyde & Phenol free combination of active substances (Non-toxic)
  • Non-corrosive – supplemented with anti-corrosion agents
  • In-built cleansing power; microbicidal power equivalent to Glutaraldehyde
  • No required activators, and no wastage – a working solution can be prepared as needed
  • Long shelf life (3 years)
  • Economical: 2% solution for complete disinfection of the environment within 30 minutes
  • Virucidal (tested on HIV, HBV, HCV)
  • Non-carcinogenic
  • Non-irritant
  • Longer residual effect makes it usable in discarded jars

Directions

Open the sachet and measure 20 mL of Uni-Surf. Dilute with 980 mL of water to prepare a 1-liter, 2% solution. Apply the solution using a spray, mop, or cloth to clean and disinfect washable surfaces. The solution is effective within 15–30 minutes.

Uni-Surf Fogging Use Instructions

Area Diluted Solution Fogging Time
304 m³ (1000 ft) 20 mL Uni-Surf + 980 mL Water 15 Min.
608 m³ (2000 ft) 40 mL Uni-Surf + 1960 mL Water 25 Min.
914 m³ (3000 ft) 80 mL Uni-Surf + 2920 mL Water 45 Min.

Suitable for hospitals, outpatient clinics, food industries, restaurants, veterinary hospitals, airports, and transportation.

Caution

Avoid contact with eyes. In case of contact, rinse with plenty of water. Do not mix with other cleaners or disinfectants. Keep out of reach of children, and glove usage is recommended.
