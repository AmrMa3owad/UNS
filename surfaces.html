Unicid Spray

UniCid Spray is a fast-acting, ready-to-use disinfectant spray designed for dental and medical surfaces. It is effective against all bacteria (including TB), viruses (including HIV, HBV, HCV, and MRSA), and fungi.

With a rapid action time of 30 seconds to 1 minute, it is ideal for use in dental clinics, ENT clinics, ICUs, operating rooms, emergency rooms, isolation units, hemodialysis rooms, and for managing MDR patients.

Directions

  1. Spray the surface from a distance of approximately 25 cm.
  2. Wait for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  3. Wipe the surface with clean wipes.

Composition

Contains 70% alcohol (Ethanol and IPA), Benzalkonium Chloride, Didecyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride, and Chlorhexidine Gluconate.

Microbiologically tested by: CDCF- Egypt (Code 3038)

Unicid Free

UniCid Free is an alcohol-free disinfectant and cleaner formulated for medical and dental surfaces. It is especially gentle on delicate materials such as genuine and synthetic leather, acrylics, and other surfaces sensitive to alcohol.

Designed for effectiveness against bacteria, fungi, and viruses, UniCid Free can be applied via a ready-to-use spray bottle or as a fogging solution with a compatible machine.

Other General Uses

This versatile product is also ideal for use in fitness centers, offices, kitchens, homes, schools, restaurants, and any other setting where alcohol-sensitive surfaces are present.

Directions

  1. Spray the surface or objects from approximately 30 cm away to ensure complete coverage.
  2. Allow the disinfectant to act for 60 seconds until it evaporates (for full efficacy, leave for 5 minutes).
  3. Let the surface dry naturally, or wipe it with a clean cloth or tissue.

Composition

Contains Benzalkonium Chloride, Didecyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride, surfactants, auxiliary agents, and water.

Caution

Avoid contact with eyes and skin; if contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water. Do not mix with other cleaners or disinfectants, and keep out of reach of children.

Efficacy & Contact Times (Undiluted)

  • Disinfectant Spray: Bactericidal (including Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Enterococcus, Staphylococcus aureus, MRSA, VRE) in 60 seconds.
  • Bactericidal (including TBC), Yeasticidal (Candida Albicans), and Fungicidal (Aspergillus niger) in 1 minute.
  • Mycobactericidal (Mycobacterium avium) in 2 minutes.
  • Hospital pathogens such as Legionella pneumophila and Clostridium difficile spores in 5 minutes.
  • Enveloped viruses (2–5 minutes), including blood-borne viruses such as Hepatitis B (HBV), Hepatitis C (HCV), Hepatitis Delta (HDV), HIV, Influenza (including H1N1, H5N1), Coronavirus, herpes simplex, Filoviridae (Ebola, Marburg), BVDV, vaccinia, and other coronaviruses (e.g., MERS, SARS).
