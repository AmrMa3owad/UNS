Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to the most common questions and learn more about our products.

How can we use UniCid Forte?

Add 100 ML of UniCid forte to 900 ML water to make 1 Li ready to use and soak it in ultrasonic cleaner for 5 Minutes only. Or add 50 ML UniCid forte to 950 ML water to make 1 Li ready to use and soak it container manually for 30 Minutes.

UniJet DD what for use?

UniJet DD is a perfect solution for Dental suction use and maintenance. It has triple effects (clean, disinfectant, and deodorize).

Are the products being safe for users?

All our products are safe and friendly for all users if follow the instructions of uses.

What is the shelf life for UniCid Forte after diluted?

With normal use we can use it for 5 days after dilute but in case the solution color changed or there is a lot of blood we must change it immediately.

What is the difference between Unicid Spray and Unicid Free?

The two products is the same effects on surfaces but with deferent composition ( UniCid Free is water based and UniCid Spray is Alcohol based) so the effect time is deferent. Also, if we will spray screen, control panel, laser we recommended Free.
