Hexiroot 2%

A highly effective formulation for root canal irrigation, enhanced with a refreshing mint flavor and surfactants.

Designed exclusively for dental professionals, Hexiroot 2% ensures thorough irrigation of the root canal system while effectively combating all pathogens. Use strictly as directed by a qualified dentist or endodontist.

Active Ingredient: 2% Chlorhexidine Gluconate.

Warnings: For professional use only. Not for oral consumption. Avoid contact with eyes and skin; if contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water. Keep out of reach of children. Store at room temperature and protect from light.

Hyporoot 2%

The gold standard in root canal irrigation, this solution combines a refreshing mint infusion with the powerful antibacterial and tissue-dissolving properties of 2.5% Sodium Hypochlorite (NaOCl).

Designed exclusively for dental professionals, this irrigant ensures thorough cleansing of the root canal system. Use strictly as directed by a qualified dentist or endodontist.

Active Ingredient: 2.5% Sodium Hypochlorite (NaOCl).

Directions: Follow your dentist's instructions for proper application.

Warnings: For professional use only. Not for oral consumption. Avoid contact with eyes and skin; if contact occurs, rinse immediately with plenty of water. Keep out of reach of children. Store at room temperature and protect from light.

Hyporoot 5.25%

Featuring 5.25% Sodium Hypochlorite (NaOCl), the gold standard for root canal irrigants thanks to its superior antibacterial and tissue-dissolving properties.

This product is exclusively for dental professionals and is designed to thoroughly irrigate the root canal system. Use only as directed by a qualified dentist or endodontist.

Active Ingredient: 5.25% Sodium Hypochlorite (NaOCl).

Directions: Follow your dentist's instructions for proper application.

Warnings: For professional use only. Not for oral consumption. Avoid contact with eyes and skin; if contact occurs, rinse immediately with plenty of water. Keep out of reach of children. Store at room temperature and protect from light.
