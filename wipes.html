UniCavi Wipes

Fast Ready-to-Use Disinfectant Wipes for Hard Surfaces

UniCavi Wipes are very fast acting disinfectant wipes designed for all hard medical and dental surfaces. They provide broad-spectrum efficacy against bacteria including TB and MRSA, viruses including HIV, HBV, HCV and Coronavirus, and fungi.

Characteristics

  • Fast acting – 60 seconds contact time
  • Free of aldehydes and phenols
  • Ready to use

Directions

Wipe the surface thoroughly and allow to act for 1 minute.

Applicable In

  • Dental & ENT Clinics
  • ICU, OR, ER, Isolation Rooms
  • Hemodialysis Rooms
  • MDR patient areas

Composition: 22.5% Alcohol (Ethanol & IPA), Didecyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride, other ingredients.

Storage: Store at room temperature in a dry place. Keep away from heat and ignition sources.

UniCid Free Wipes

Alcohol-Free Surface Disinfectant & Cleaner

UniCid Free Wipes are specially formulated for delicate and alcohol-sensitive surfaces such as leather, acrylics, and similar materials. They provide effective disinfection while being gentle on sensitive surfaces.

Characteristics

  • Alcohol-free formula
  • Safe for delicate and sensitive surfaces
  • Fast acting (1–2 minutes)

Applicable In

  • Medical & Dental Clinics
  • Offices, Schools, Restaurants
  • Homes & Kitchens
  • Fitness Centers

Composition: Benzalkonium Chloride, Didecyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride.

Contact Time: 1–2 minutes

Storage: Store at room temperature in a dry place. Keep out of reach of children.

UniCid Wipes

Broad Spectrum Disinfectant & Cleaning Wipes

UniCid Wipes are fast acting disinfectant and detergent wipes for medical and dental surfaces. They are effective against bacteria including TB and MRSA, viruses, and fungi.

Characteristics

  • Fast acting – 30 seconds
  • Broad spectrum efficacy
  • Free of aldehydes and phenols

Directions

Wipe the surface and allow a contact time of 30 seconds.

Applicable In

  • Dental & ENT Clinics
  • ICU, OR, ER, Isolation Rooms
  • Hemodialysis Rooms

Composition: 70% Alcohol (Ethanol & IPA), Benzalkonium Chloride, Didecyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride, Chlorhexidine Gluconate.

Storage: Store at room temperature in a dry place. Not recommended for alcohol-sensitive surfaces.

IPA 70% Wipes

Multi-purpose Disinfectant Wipes for General & Medical Use

IPA 70% Wipes are ready-to-use isopropyl alcohol wipes designed for fast and effective disinfection, cleaning, and antiseptic use on skin and hard surfaces. Suitable for both general and medical applications.

Directions for Use

  • Wipe the affected area thoroughly
  • Allow the solution to air dry
  • For skin use, wipe gently and thoroughly
  • Contact time: 30 seconds

Uses

  • Disinfectant
  • Cleaning agent
  • Antiseptic for skin and surfaces

Ingredients: Isopropyl Alcohol 70%, Water.

Product Details

  • Wipe Size: 21 cm / 35 cm (JUMBO)
  • Pack Size: 140 wipes
  • Lot No: Wi001

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep container tightly closed when not in use

Cautions

  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Flammable – keep away from heat, sparks, and open flames
  • Do not ingest
  • In case of eye contact, rinse immediately with plenty of water and seek medical attention
