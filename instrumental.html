Unicid Forte

UniCid Forte is a specialized concentrate formulated for the effective disinfection and cleaning of various instruments used in hospitals as well as in general medical and dental clinics. It is ideal for use on diamond drills, rubber burnishers, root canal instruments, forceps, and for both pre-operative and post-operative disinfection of instruments such as endoscopes, diagnostic tools, surgical devices, preserving equipment, and anesthesia accessories. This product is compatible with instruments made of metal, porcelain, glass, stainless steel, plastic, and rubber.

Its unique blend of active substances and surfactants is non-reactive with medical or dental instruments during the disinfection process, offering broad-spectrum efficacy against bacteria (including tuberculosis), fungi, a variety of viruses (including HIV and HBV), coronaviruses, and MRSA. Economical in use, only a 10% solution is required for immersion disinfection in an instrument holding tray (with an action time ranging from 5 to 60 minutes), or in an ultrasonic cleaning device (achieving disinfection in just 5 minutes).

Characteristics

  • Light blue, clear liquid free of aldehydes and phenols
  • Pleasant scent
  • Non-corrosive and biodegradable

Application

Prepare a 10% solution and use it in an instrument holding tray for 5–60 minutes, or in an ultrasonic cleaning device for 5 minutes.

Directions

  1. Remove the dosing cap and fill the dosing section with 100 ml of UniCid Forte by gently squeezing the bottle.
  2. Dilute the 100 ml with 900 ml of water to create 1 liter of a ready-to-use 10% solution (the diluted solution is effective for up to 3 days).
  3. Immerse the instrument for the appropriate time as per the following guidelines:
    • Viruses (HBV/HIV): 10% solution for 5 minutes
    • Bacteria & fungi: 10% solution for 60 minutes
    • Tuberculosis Bacillus: 10% solution for 60 minutes
  4. After immersion, rinse the instruments under running water, dry them with an air jet or paper towel, and then proceed to sterilization.
  5. Replace the solution once or twice a week, or daily depending on the level of contamination.

Active Ingredients: Didecyldimethyl Ammonium Chloride, Benzalkonium Chloride, emulsifying agent, surfactants, and auxiliary agent.

Density: Approximately 1.00 g/ml
pH (undiluted): Approximately 9.5
Storage: Store in a cool, dry place.
